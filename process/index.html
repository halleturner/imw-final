Inspiration

I wanted my final website to reflect my love of drawing and journaling so I envisioned a virtual sketchbook as my point of reference. I then started looking at other websites that inspired me with their graphics, particularly, I drew inspiration from the Interactive Media Web page, Moonshine.mu and last year's student Abha. There were elements in each of these pages that I enjoyed for various reasons.

Font wise, I wanted something fun and approachable so I went with a font that was slightly rounded but not too much otherwise I feared it might look dated. I ran into many challenges when trying to iterate my design that by the end of the process I determined less is more with my concept hence why I mainly utilized css and a small bit of javascript for the more in-depth interactions.

Final Ideations

In the end, I designed an interactive website where upon entering the user can draw on the screen any image they want and then they can click on the side tabs that lead to the informative sections of the website.
